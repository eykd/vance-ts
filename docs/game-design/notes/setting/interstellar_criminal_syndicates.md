# Interstellar Criminal Syndicates — Oikumene & Beyond

---

## A. Structure & Scope
- **Cartels:** Multi-system operations controlling commodities (e.g., spice, rare isotopes, illegal biotech) from source to sale.
- **Syndicates:** Decentralized networks of allied crews and bosses sharing resources, protection, and markets.
- **Franchise Crews:** Small groups using a major syndicate’s name, reputation, and protection for a cut.
- **Shadow Guilds:** Criminal counterparts to legitimate guilds, running parallel markets in forbidden goods/services.

---

## B. Core Activities
- **Smuggling:** Moving banned goods, people, or data via covert corridors.
- **Debt Enforcement:** Outside treaty law, using coercion, assassination, or hostage-taking.
- **Vice Industries:** Gambling, narcotics, flesh trade, immersive simulation dens.
- **Black Contract Work:** Piracy, sabotage, assassination-for-hire masked as private commerce.

---

## C. Territories & Safe Havens
- **Beyond Strongholds:** Stations and asteroid bases beyond Oikumene jurisdiction; defended like capitals.
- **Corrupt Ports:** Oikumene worlds with officials on syndicate payrolls.
- **Mobile Havens:** Giant ships serving as floating bazaars and neutral grounds.

---

## D. Relationships with Power
- **Pact Diplomacy:** Truces with guilds, blocs, or warlords for mutual benefit.
- **Proxy Warfare:** Acting as deniable arms of legitimate powers.
- **Church Opposition & Exploitation:** Missions resist vices, but syndicates launder funds through donations.

---

## E. Beyond Practices
- **Debt Wafers:** Bio-coded obligations payable only through syndicate-approved channels.
- **Ritualized Vendettas:** Feuds fought under elaborate underworld codes.
- **Patron Saints of Vice:** Cultic veneration of legendary crime lords.

---

## F. Narrative Leverage
- Debt wafer shipment intercepted, destabilizing multiple black economies.
- Syndicate-controlled port hosts both Church missionaries and a guild delegation.
- Rival syndicates wage proxy war in a corridor critical to Oikumene trade.
