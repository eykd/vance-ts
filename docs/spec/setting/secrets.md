# Secrets

> Societal secrets as narrative currency — hidden knowledge that drives discovery and progression.

## Overview

Secrets are a narrative design pattern from Failbetter Games where hidden knowledge functions as a game resource. Societies, factions, and locations all have secrets — discovering them creates narrative depth, drives progression, and provides trading currency.

## Design Intent

- Secrets give exploration a concrete reward beyond loot
- Knowledge asymmetry creates interesting player-to-player dynamics in MMO
- Secrets layer onto cultures generated by PCG — every society has something hidden
- Discovery of secrets can unlock storylets, faction access, and trade opportunities
