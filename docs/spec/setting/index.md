# Setting Index

The baptized-Vance galaxy: a civilized network of systems (the Oikumene) contrasting with the mysterious Beyond.

## World Structure

1. **[The Oikumene](oikumene.md)** — The civilized spiral arm: trade routes, law, factions, culture. Knowable, navigable, politically complex.

2. **[The Beyond](beyond.md)** — Everything outside the Oikumene: mystery, danger, discovery. Procedurally deep, narratively surprising.

## Social Fabric

3. **[Factions](factions.md)** — Major powers, merchant houses, criminal syndicates, religious orders. The political landscape players navigate.

4. **[Culture PCG](culture-pcg.md)** — Procedural culture generation using Holly Lisle's method. How we create culturally distinct worlds within the Oikumene milieu.

5. **[Secrets](secrets.md)** — Societal secrets as narrative currency. How hidden knowledge drives discovery and progression.

## Physical World

6. **[Planet & System PCG](planet-pcg.md)** — Procedural world generation pipeline. Star types, planet characteristics, trade codes.

## Creative Direction

7. **[Tone & Style](tone-and-style.md)** — Vancean voice, writing guidelines, prose style for all game text.

8. **[History](history.md)** — Timeline, eras, and formative events that shaped the Oikumene.

## Concept Art

9. **[Concept Art Index](concept-art/index.md)** — Exemplar planets and culture dossiers that demonstrate the range, theme, and possibility space of the procedural generation system. These are not canonical locations — they're design targets showing what PCG should be capable of producing.
