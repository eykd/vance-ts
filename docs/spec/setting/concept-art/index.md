# Concept Art

> Exemplar planets and cultures demonstrating the range and possibility space of PCG.

## What This Is

These are **not canonical locations**. They are design targets — "concept art" in document form — that show what the procedural culture and planet generation systems should be capable of producing. They demonstrate the range of themes, the depth of cultural detail, and the Vancean voice that PCG output should aim for.

## How to Use These

- **For PCG design**: These exemplars define the quality bar. If PCG can't produce something at this level, the system needs work.
- **For writing**: These show the target voice and level of cultural detail for generated text.
- **For systems design**: Each dossier implies mechanical behaviors (what would the meter curves be? what storylets would fire here?).
