# Factions

> Major powers, merchant houses, criminal syndicates, and religious orders.

## Overview

The Oikumene's political landscape is defined by competing factions — each with distinct cultures, values, and mechanical behaviors. Factions are where setting meets systems: each faction's cultural identity translates into unique meter curves, trade modifiers, and storylet spaces.
