# The Oikumene

> The civilized galaxy: a network of systems connected by trade routes along a spiral arm.

## Overview

The Oikumene is the known, civilized space — a web of inhabited systems connected by established jump routes. It's politically complex, culturally diverse, and economically active. Players begin here as tramp traders navigating its trade network. The Oikumene is _knowable_ — with time and effort, you can learn its routes, politics, and rhythms.
