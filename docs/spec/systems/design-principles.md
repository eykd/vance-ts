# Design Principles

> Engagement strategy, pacing, and anti-patterns to avoid.

## Overview

Guiding principles that apply across all systems. These are the "why" behind design decisions.

## Engagement Strategy (Octalysis Framework)

Majoring on white-hat core drives for sustainable, intrinsic motivation:

| Core Drive                            | Role                                                                                         | Priority |
| ------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | -------- |
| **CD1: Epic Meaning**                 | You're a captain carving your path through a vast galaxy; the Beyond holds genuine mysteries | High     |
| **CD2: Development & Accomplishment** | Ship upgrades, crew growth, faction reputation, route mastery                                | High     |
| **CD3: Empowerment of Creativity**    | Route planning, trade strategy, crew composition, quest choices                              | High     |
| **CD5: Social Influence**             | MMO interactions, faction politics, visible reputation                                       | Medium   |
| **CD7: Unpredictability & Curiosity** | QBN encounters, the Beyond's surprises, emergent narrative                                   | Medium   |

Minimizing black-hat techniques:

| Core Drive                     | Stance                                                                                                 |
| ------------------------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **CD6: Scarcity & Impatience** | Limit to diegetic scarcity (fuel, cargo space) — no artificial energy timers or premium currency gates |
| **CD8: Loss & Avoidance**      | Ship damage and reputation loss should feel consequential but recoverable — never punitive             |

## Turn-Based Pacing

- Each turn represents a meaningful decision
- No real-time pressure — players engage at their own pace
- MMO aspects work asynchronously

## Narrative Design Principles

- QBN-driven: encounters emerge from player state, not scripts
- No linear main quest — progression comes from expanding reach
- The Oikumene is knowable; the Beyond rewards exploration with discovery
- Emergent narrative from systems, not authored content (essential for sustainability)

## Anti-Patterns to Avoid

- Binary pass/fail challenges (use yarnball spectrum instead)
- Stat inflation masquerading as progression
- Content treadmill (use emergence instead)
- Artificial scarcity / energy timers
- Punitive loss mechanics
- Optimization puzzles with single solutions (yarnball should resist "solving")
