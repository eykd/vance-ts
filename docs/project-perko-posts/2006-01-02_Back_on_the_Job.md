## Monday, January 02, 2006 


### Back on the Job

I've started to dive back into Machine City. Going will be slow for a few days - perhaps as long as two weeks - while I negotiate with my new life. (I have a *chair* now! How cool is that?) However, I do expect that [the Machine City blog](http://themachinecity.blogspot.com/) will become my "primary" (or, at least, most audience-friendly) blog. It'll also be fun to watch the evolution of a game, don't you think?  
  
So if you don't have it on your feed, add it! [The Machine City blog](http://themachinecity.blogspot.com/) awaits you, with posts of substance and implementation, as opposed to the ivory tower bull you get here!  
  
Also, posts over there tend to be shorter and have pictures in them. :)
