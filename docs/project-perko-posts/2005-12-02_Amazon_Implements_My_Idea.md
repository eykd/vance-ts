## Friday, December 02, 2005 


### Amazon Implements My Idea!

I don't think they copied it from me, but nonetheless, I had the idea before they released it. I suggested it as a Google thing.  
  
"What the hell are you talking about?"  
  
[Amazon's new "Artificial Artificial Intelligence" system](http://www.mturk.com/mturk/welcome).  
  
Which acts quite a lot like [my idea](http://projectperko.blogspot.com/2005/08/pattern-recognition-redux.html) . Except, of course, that Amazon is using *money* , where I wanted to use *psychology*.  
  
My explicit idea was the [Google Search Game](http://projectperko.blogspot.com/2005/08/google-search-game.html), but Amazon didn't go that far with their "Mechanical Turk". Still, I'm going to pretend that's a flaw on their part, rather than mine. :D  
  
Whee!
