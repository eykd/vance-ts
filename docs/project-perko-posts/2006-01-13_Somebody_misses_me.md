## Friday, January 13, 2006 


### Somebody misses me!

I knew that *some*body would miss me when I moved away from Seattle, and I was right. Since literally the day I moved out, Seattle itself has been crying non-stop. 26 days of rain, straight.  
  
Buck up, Seattle. We had good times, but we knew it was never meant to be.
