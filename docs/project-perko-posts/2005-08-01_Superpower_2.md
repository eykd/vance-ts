## Monday, August 01, 2005 


### Superpower 2

I BOUGHT this game. For $30. From Fry's. I install it, and it asks me to enter the CD key.  
  
CD key?  
  
There IS NO CD KEY. Neither of the CDs, no place on the jewel case, no part of the box, in no help file. There IS NO CD KEY.  
  
Now, I try finding a crack, and I can't find a fucking crack, either!  
  
Even better, the "Contact" section AND forum section of their site are busted.  
  
FUCK YOU. I like Dreamworks, but whoever did their production really FUCKED THINGS UP.
