## Tuesday, November 01, 2005 


### The Machine City

I'm starting up a new blog over at [Machine City, the Blog](http://themachinecity.blogspot.com/) specifically for The Machine City. It's far enough along that I have confidence I'll be finishing the product, and I don't want to clutter it up with my random unrelated posts.  
  
If you have any interest in seeing the trials and tribulations of an indie dev working on a moderately large indie game, get the feed and enjoy.  
  
It's gonna have a LOT of screenshots and applied theory. :)
