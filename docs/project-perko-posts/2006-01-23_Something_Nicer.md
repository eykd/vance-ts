## Monday, January 23, 2006 


### Something Nicer...

To keep the rant from being my front post, here's a little something:  
  
[Seth Godin made a throw-away post](http://sethgodin.typepad.com/seths_blog/2006/01/fear_and_creati.html) about fear. Actually, several of his recent posts have been about fear, but this is the only one I'm gonna chime in on.  
  
"Creativity" may not be an enemy of fear, but I'm pretty sure "adaptability" is. And adaptability is, after all, deeply linked to creativity. :)
