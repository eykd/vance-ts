## Thursday, May 11, 2006 


### Are you surprised?

[The Government is watching you.](http://www.cnn.com/2006/POLITICS/05/11/nsa.phonerecords.ap/index.html) Are you surprised?  
  
I talked to an astounding number of people about this over the past five years. Many of them said, "They're just tracking suspects! If you're a normal person..."  
  
I always called bullshit, and now it turns out I've been right.  
  
This doesn't affect me any directly - my phone calls are completely innocuous and, frankly, I have nothing to hide. However, I don't trust them to stop with phone calls, just as I didn't trust them to stop with suspicious people's phone calls. I don't trust them to stop at monitoring, I don't trust them to stop at records, and I don't trust them to limit themselves to phones.  
  
If my vote mattered (and don't argue with me on this: it doesn't) I would vote for whoever ran on an "anti-security" platform for president.
