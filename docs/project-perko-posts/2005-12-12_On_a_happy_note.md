## Monday, December 12, 2005 


### On a happy note...

You know, every year I like Penny Arcade more. They seem to get more subtle and talented each year. But, for some reason, I doubt "the crowd" feels the same way. For example, I consider [this comic](http://www.penny-arcade.com/comic/2005/12/09) to be impressively subtle and interesting, but how many 15-year-old geek boys will feel that way?  
  
Hm. I wonder if they made the choice to appeal to a more mature crowd, or whether that's simply the direction they subconsciously go...
