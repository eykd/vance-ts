## Monday, June 11, 2007 


### A Few Suggestions

I love comics and weird RPGs. If you love either/or, here are some very unique ones you have to read or play, hyperlinked to as good a starting place as I can find. These are all obscure: give yourself a point for having heard of one, or three points for having read it/studied the manual.  
  
Comics:  


*  [Platinum Grit](http://www.platinumgrit.com/issue08.htm)  
  

*  [Finder](http://www.lightspeedpress.com/index.php?module=Finder&func=pub&issue=19&page=1)  
  

*  [Thieves and Kings](http://www.glasswings.com.au/comics/t+k/quintonstrips/sample_comic.htm)  
  

*  [Empowered](http://www.darkhorse.com/profile/profile.php?sku=14-043) (Hey, it's his best work.)  
  
RPGs:  


*  [Nobilis](http://www.ogrecave.com/reviews/nobilis.shtml)  
  

*  [Amber](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Amber_Diceless_Roleplaying_Game)  
  

*  [PrimeTime Adventures](http://www.rpg.net/reviews/archive/10/10903.phtml)  
  

*  [With Great Power](http://www.rpg.net/reviews/archive/11/11606.phtml)  
  
Points:  
0-1: Not very geeky, man.  
2-4: Big geek.  
5-8: Top notch indie nerd.  
9-15: You already heard about these in person from me.  
16+: There is no 16+.  
  
All of these have very unique elements to them, so in addition to being fun, I think they are very good to learn from.
