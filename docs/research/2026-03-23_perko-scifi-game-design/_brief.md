# Research Brief

## Research Question

What are Craig Perko's key insights on sci-fi game design, focusing on: space survival mechanics, CRPG design patterns, location/world design, and NPC interaction systems?

## Context

Building a text-only MMORPG set in a Vance-inspired galaxy. Need to extract Perko's design philosophy from his 2005–2019 blog archive (1,578 posts indexed in QMD `perko` collection) to inform our game design decisions.

## Priority Aspects

- Practical design patterns and mechanics
- Expert opinions on what works/doesn't work
- Historical examples from games Perko analyzes

## Output Format

Structured briefing

## Complexity Tier

Complex (4 distinct search angles)

## Done Criteria

1. At least 3 distinct Perko insights on space/survival game mechanics
2. At least 3 insights on CRPG design (character progression, skills, choices)
3. At least 3 insights on location/world design (procedural generation, exploration, sense of place)
4. At least 3 insights on NPC interaction design (dialogue, relationships, crew dynamics)
5. Cross-cutting themes identified across the four angles

## Search Angles

1. Space survival, resource management, ship mechanics
2. CRPG design, character systems, player choice
3. Location design, procedural worlds, exploration
4. NPC interaction, dialogue systems, social mechanics
