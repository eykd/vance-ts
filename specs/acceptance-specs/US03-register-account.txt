;=============================================
; A new visitor can register an account.
;=============================================
GIVEN a new visitor is on the registration page.
WHEN they submit a valid email address and a strong password.
THEN their account is created.
THEN they are redirected to the sign-in page.
THEN the sign-in page acknowledges that registration was successful.

;=============================================
; Registering with an email already in use silently redirects to sign-in.
;=============================================
GIVEN an account already exists with a given email address.
WHEN a visitor submits the registration form with that same email address.
THEN they are redirected to the sign-in page.
THEN the page gives no indication that the email address was already registered.

;=============================================
; A password shorter than 12 characters is rejected.
;=============================================
GIVEN a new visitor is on the registration page.
WHEN they submit a valid email address and a password shorter than 12 characters.
THEN they remain on the registration page.
THEN they see an error explaining the minimum password length requirement.

;=============================================
; A commonly used password is rejected.
;=============================================
GIVEN a new visitor is on the registration page.
WHEN they submit a valid email address and a commonly used password.
THEN they remain on the registration page.
THEN they see an error explaining that the password is too common.

;=============================================
; Registration is blocked after too many attempts.
;=============================================
GIVEN a visitor has exceeded the allowed number of registration attempts.
WHEN they attempt to register again.
THEN they are told to wait before trying again.
