;=============================================
; A registered user can sign in.
;=============================================
GIVEN a registered user is on the sign-in page.
WHEN they submit their email address and correct password.
THEN they are signed in and redirected to the application.

;=============================================
; An incorrect password shows a generic error.
;=============================================
GIVEN a registered user is on the sign-in page.
WHEN they submit their email address with an incorrect password.
THEN they remain on the sign-in page.
THEN they see a generic error that does not reveal whether the email exists.

;=============================================
; An unregistered email shows the same generic error.
;=============================================
GIVEN a visitor is on the sign-in page.
WHEN they submit an email address with no registered account.
THEN they remain on the sign-in page.
THEN they see the same generic error as for an incorrect password.

;=============================================
; Sign-in is blocked after too many failed attempts.
;=============================================
GIVEN a visitor has exceeded the allowed number of sign-in attempts.
WHEN they attempt to sign in again.
THEN they are told to wait before trying again.

;=============================================
; The original destination is preserved through sign-in.
;=============================================
GIVEN an unauthenticated visitor attempts to access a protected page.
WHEN they are redirected to the sign-in page and sign in successfully.
THEN they are sent to the page they originally requested.
