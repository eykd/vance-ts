# Posts

Blog posts section. Individual posts are markdown files with frontmatter.

## Frontmatter Fields

- `title` - Post title (required)
- `description` - SEO description and summary
- `date` - Publication date (YYYY-MM-DD or ISO 8601)
- `tags` - Array of tag strings for categorization
- `toc` - Boolean to enable table of contents
- `draft` - Boolean to exclude from builds
