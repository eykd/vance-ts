## What Is ClawTask?

ClawTask is the operating system that turns you and your AI into a disciplined OKR execution team. We help you set clear objectives, track key results, and stay focused on what matters most.

## Our Mission

We believe that the best results come from disciplined execution. ClawTask combines the power of AI with proven goal-setting frameworks to help individuals and teams achieve more with less friction.

## How It Works

ClawTask pairs you with an AI partner that understands your goals and helps you break them down into actionable steps. By combining OKR methodology with intelligent task management, we keep you on track and moving forward.

## Built by Worlds Enough Studios

ClawTask is built by Worlds Enough Studios in the Pacific Northwest. We are passionate about building tools that help people do their best work.
