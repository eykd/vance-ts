## Set goals. Execute with AI. Stay on track.

ClawTask pairs you with an AI partner that understands OKR methodology and keeps you focused on the work that matters most. Define your objectives, break them into key results, and let your AI co-pilot help you execute — step by step.

### Why ClawTask?

- **Disciplined execution, not just goal setting.** Most OKR tools stop at tracking. ClawTask actively helps you break down key results into actionable tasks and checks in on your progress.
- **AI that understands your goals.** Your AI partner learns the context behind your objectives so it can suggest next steps, flag risks, and keep momentum going.
- **Less friction, more focus.** Stop juggling spreadsheets, project boards, and chat threads. ClawTask brings your goals, tasks, and AI assistant into one place.

### How It Works

1. **Define your objectives.** Set clear, measurable OKRs that align your work with what matters.
2. **Break them down.** ClawTask helps you decompose key results into concrete tasks you can act on today.
3. **Execute with your AI partner.** Your AI co-pilot stays with you through execution — surfacing priorities, tracking progress, and adapting when plans change.
