# FAQ / Q&A Structure

**Core principle**: Organize content around discrete reader questions for targeted, non-linear consumption.

## Why It Works

Matches how users naturally seek informationâ€”specific questions, direct answers. Reduces cognitive load through targeting. Creates scannability. FAQ schema enables featured snippets and "People Also Ask" visibility.

## When to Use

- Product/service pages addressing concerns
- Pre-purchase decision support
- Knowledge bases
- Controversial/misunderstood topics
- Reducing support tickets

## When NOT to Use

- Content requiring narrative flow
- Complex topics needing progressive understanding
- Deep-dive analysis
- When questions feel forced

## Template

```
INTRODUCTION (100-150 words):
Brief context, how to use, table of contents with jump links

CATEGORY A: [TOPIC] (5-7 questions)

Q: [Direct question readers actually ask]?
A: [2-3 sentence direct answer]
   [Expanded context paragraph if needed]
   [Link to deeper resource]

Q: [Next question]?
A: [Direct answer]
   [Comparison table if applicable]
   [Link to full details]

[Continue pattern]

CATEGORY B: [TOPIC]
[6-8 questions, same structure]

CATEGORY C: [TOPIC]
[5-6 questions]

DIDN'T FIND YOUR ANSWER?
Contact form, question submission

ADDITIONAL RESOURCES:
Links to guides, tools, community
```

## Variations

- **Single-topic deep-dive**: 8-12 questions, detailed answers
- **Q&A interview**: Expert quotes preserved
- **Problem-solution FAQ**: Troubleshooting focus

## Best For

SaaS features, e-commerce products, healthcare patient education, legal/financial questions, travel guides.
