# Case Study Structure

**Core principle**: Results-focused narrative documenting customer success with specific, measurable outcomes.

## Why It Works

78% of B2B businesses use case studies (Forbes Advisor). Combines social proof, narrative engagement, relatability, and specificity. 60% of marketers rank case studies among most trusted content types.

## When to Use

- B2B services and software
- High-value purchases
- Complex solutions requiring proof
- Building credibility in new market segments
- Sales enablement

## When NOT to Use

- Consumer products with short consideration cycles
- Can't share specific metrics
- Early-stage without success stories
- Commoditized products

## Template

```
HERO SECTION:
- Customer logo
- Key stat callout: "197% increase in [metric]"
- Industry/size tags

H2: Executive Summary (100-150 words)
The challenge, the solution, key results (3 bullets)

H2: About [Customer Company]
Industry, size, business context, relevant background

H2: The Challenge
- Pain points before solution
- Business impact of problems
- Why previous solutions failed
- Customer quote about challenges

H2: Why They Chose [Your Solution]
Selection criteria, what stood out, concerns addressed

H2: The Solution
Implementation details, key features utilized, timeline

H2: The Results
H3: [Metric 1]: [XX]% Improvement
Specific data, context, visual chart
H3: [Metric 2]: [XX]% Improvement
[Repeat for key metrics]

H3: Qualitative Benefits
Team satisfaction, operational improvements, unexpected wins

H2: Customer Testimonial
Video embed or detailed quote with photo

H2: Looking Ahead
Future plans, expansion opportunities

CONCLUSION:
Summary of transformation
CTA: "See how we can help your [industry]"
```

## Variations

- **Video case study**: Testimonial-driven
- **Mini case study**: 300-500 words, results-focused
- **Comparison case study**: vs. competitor solution
- **Series**: Multiple customers, same industry

## Best For

B2B SaaS, marketing agencies, consulting, manufacturing, healthcare, financial services.

## Length

Typically 1,000-2,000 words.
