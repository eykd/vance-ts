# Comparison / Versus Structure

**Core principle**: Side-by-side evaluation of competing options with clear recommendations by use case.

## Why It Works

Captures bottom-of-funnel trafficâ€”prospects actively deciding. BOFU posts convert at 4.78% vs TOFU at 0.19% (25x difference). Comparison keywords convert at >7.5%, nearly 4x average landing page rates.

## When to Use

- Software differentiation
- High-consideration purchases
- Competitive positioning
- Affiliate marketing

## When NOT to Use

- Can't demonstrate genuine product testing
- Competitor significantly superior
- Products without clear differentiators
- Can't maintain/update regularly

## Template

```
QUICK VERDICT BOX:
- TL;DR recommendation
- Best for [use case 1]: Product A
- Best for [use case 2]: Product B

OVERVIEW COMPARISON TABLE:
| Feature    | Product A | Product B |
| Pricing    | $X/mo     | $Y/mo     |
[8-12 key features with checkmarks]

H2: What Is [Product A]?
Company background, value proposition, target audience, screenshot

H2: What Is [Product B]?
[Same structure]

H2: Feature-by-Feature Comparison
H3: [Feature Category 1]
How each handles it, winner with reasoning
H3: [Feature Category 2]
[Repeat for all key features]

H2: Pricing Comparison
Detailed breakdown, hidden costs, value analysis

H2: Pros and Cons
H3: [Product A] Pros and Cons
H3: [Product B] Pros and Cons

H2: Who Should Choose [Product A]?
Ideal user profiles, specific use cases

H2: Who Should Choose [Product B]?
[Same structure]

H2: Our Testing Methodology
How we evaluated (builds E-E-A-T)

FAQ SECTION (with schema markup)

CONCLUSION:
Final recommendation by scenario, CTA
```

## Variations

- **Three-way comparison**
- **Alternatives roundup**: "10 Best [Competitor] Alternatives"
- **Category comparison**: Comparing approaches, not products
- **Migration guide**: Switching from A to B

## Best For

SaaS/software, consumer electronics, financial products, e-commerce, professional services.

## Length

Typically 1,500-2,500 words.
