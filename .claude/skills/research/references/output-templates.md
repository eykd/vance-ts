# Output Templates for Research Synthesis

Use the template matching the user's chosen output format when writing `synthesis.md`.

---

## 1. Structured Briefing (default)

```markdown
# Research Briefing: [Topic]

**Date**: [YYYY-MM-DD]
**Research question**: [question]

## Executive Summary

[2–3 paragraph overview of findings. Lead with the answer to the research question, then key supporting evidence, then important caveats.]

## Key Findings

### [Finding 1 headline]
[Explanation with inline citations: ([Source Name](url))]
- Supporting data point or quote
- Supporting data point or quote

### [Finding 2 headline]
...

### [Finding 3 headline]
...

## Consensus & Disagreement

**Areas of consensus**:
- [point] — supported by [sources]

**Areas of disagreement**:
- [point] — [Source A] says X, [Source B] says Y

**Emerging / uncertain**:
- [point] — limited evidence, single-source, or too early to tell

## Gaps & Limitations

- [What couldn't be determined and why]
- [Areas where source quality was limited]
- [Aspects that warrant further investigation]

## Source Quality Summary

| Source | Type | Credibility | Key Contribution |
|--------|------|-------------|------------------|
| [Name](url) | [academic/news/gov/etc.] | High/Med/Low | [what it provided] |
| ... | ... | ... | ... |

## Suggested Further Research

- [Specific question or angle that emerged but wasn't fully covered]
- [Source or database to check that wasn't accessible]
```

## 2. Annotated Source List

```markdown
# Annotated Sources: [Topic]

**Date**: [YYYY-MM-DD]
**Research question**: [question]

## Overview

[1 paragraph summary of the source landscape: how many sources found, general quality, any notable patterns]

---

### [Source 1 Title]
- **URL**: [url]
- **Author/Publisher**: [name]
- **Date**: [publication date]
- **Type**: [academic paper / news article / government report / blog / etc.]
- **Credibility**: [High / Medium / Low] — [1-sentence justification]

**Relevance**: [How this source relates to the research question]

**Key quotes**:
> "[quote 1]" — [page/section if available]

> "[quote 2]"

**Key data points**:
- [data point with context]

**Limitations**: [Any caveats about this source]

---

### [Source 2 Title]
...

---

## Source Comparison

[Brief comparison of how sources relate to each other — do they corroborate, contradict, or address different aspects?]
```

## 3. Argument Map

```markdown
# Argument Map: [Topic]

**Date**: [YYYY-MM-DD]
**Central question**: [question]

## Central Claim

[State the main claim or thesis being evaluated]

## Supporting Evidence

### Evidence 1: [label]
- **Source**: [Name](url) — Credibility: [High/Med/Low]
- **Claim**: [what the source argues]
- **Strength**: [Strong / Moderate / Weak] — [why]
- **Key data**: [specific numbers, quotes, or findings]

### Evidence 2: [label]
...

## Counterevidence

### Counter 1: [label]
- **Source**: [Name](url) — Credibility: [High/Med/Low]
- **Claim**: [what the source argues against]
- **Strength**: [Strong / Moderate / Weak] — [why]
- **Key data**: [specific numbers, quotes, or findings]

### Counter 2: [label]
...

## Rebuttals & Qualifications

- [Response to Counter 1]: [what proponents say in response]
- [Response to Counter 2]: ...

## Assessment

**Weight of evidence**: [Strongly supports / Leans toward / Mixed / Leans against / Strongly against] the central claim

**Confidence level**: [High / Medium / Low]

**Key uncertainties**:
- [what would change the assessment]

**Bottom line**: [2–3 sentence synthesis]
```

## 4. Comparison Matrix

```markdown
# Comparison: [Topic]

**Date**: [YYYY-MM-DD]
**Research question**: [question]

## Options Compared

| | [Option A] | [Option B] | [Option C] |
|---|---|---|---|
| **[Criterion 1]** | [assessment] | [assessment] | [assessment] |
| **[Criterion 2]** | [assessment] | [assessment] | [assessment] |
| **[Criterion 3]** | [assessment] | [assessment] | [assessment] |
| **[Criterion 4]** | [assessment] | [assessment] | [assessment] |
| **[Criterion 5]** | [assessment] | [assessment] | [assessment] |

## Detailed Analysis

### [Criterion 1]: [label]

**[Option A]**: [detailed assessment with source citations]

**[Option B]**: [detailed assessment with source citations]

**[Option C]**: [detailed assessment with source citations]

### [Criterion 2]: [label]
...

## Trade-offs

- **[Option A]** excels at [X] but lacks [Y]
- **[Option B]** excels at [X] but lacks [Y]
- **[Option C]** excels at [X] but lacks [Y]

## Recommendation

**Best overall**: [option] — [1-sentence justification]

**Best for [specific use case]**: [option] — [why]

**Avoid if**: [conditions where a particular option is a poor fit]

## Sources

| Source | Credibility | Used For |
|--------|-------------|----------|
| [Name](url) | High/Med/Low | [what criteria it informed] |
| ... | ... | ... |
```
